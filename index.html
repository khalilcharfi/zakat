Help & Resources

Zakat is one of the Five Pillars of Islam and refers to the obligation of giving a portion of wealth to those in need. It is calculated at 2.5% of wealth that has been held for one lunar year.

Zakat is calculated at 2.5% of eligible wealth that exceeds the Nisab threshold and has been held for one lunar year (Hawl). This calculator helps determine your Zakat-eligible assets and calculates accordingly.
